Treatments

We offer a wide range of comprehensive dental services through a
modernized approach to acheive perfection and patient satisfaction

Routine Dental Care

Tooth cleaning, emergency dental care, trauma cases, tooth extractions, tooth fillings, etc.

Root Canals

A simple procedure to repair badly damaged teeth and prevent the need for dental implants.

Tooth Replacements

Fixed bridges & crowns, implants, removables such acrylic dentures,flexible dentures and cast partials.

Implants

The third set of teeth. We do single and multiple tooth implants, implant over dentures and full mouth rehabilitations with implant.

Orthodontics

Braces and clear aligners. Clear aligner is an aesthetically pleasing alternative to braces, it is gentle yet powerful as it applies the right amount of force in the right direction in a controlled manner and hence, less painful.

Aesthetic Dentistry

Smile makeovers, teeth whitening, gum depigmentation, tooth jewelery, gum surgeries, cadcam all ceramic, inlays & onlays, veneers & lumineers

Oral Surgery

All minor surgical procedures includes wisdom tooth extractions, flap surgeries, sinus lift procedures, tongue tie removal, cyst enucleations, prf/prp for faster healing, along with graft/membrane, etc.

Child Dentistry

Sealants, fluoride varnishes, stemcell banking, mouthguards, habit-breaking appliances, etc. Interceptive orthodontic treatments in children can begin as early as 5-6 years.

We use digital xrays to reduce patient radiation exposure and use 3D
scans for all our CAD/CAM restorations and clear aligner orthodontic treatments.

Sterilization Protocols

Dental treatments demand excellent infection control, and therefore we put in extra efforts to ensure patient and staff safety. We maximize the use of disposables, which prevent the chances of cross contamination. All the instruments are sealed in sterilization pouches and autoclaved in a B-class autoclave. Surface disinfectants are used on dental chairs, slabs and other surface areas. We record each of our patient's medical history and take special precaution when required. Medical waste is disposed by following State Pollution Cotrol guidelines.

    About Us

    At Olive we aim at providing comprehensive dental services with the highest standards of sterilization, quality control and radiation safety.
    In addition to providing comprehensive family dental care, our clinic is equipped with state of the art technology for advanced dental implants and cosmetic dentistry.
    Our team of highly qualified dentists and specialists is led by Dr. Jisha.

    Dr. Jisha is a highly skilled dental professional with an impressive 25-year track record in the field of dentistry. As a dedicated dental surgeon and passionate cosmetic/aesthetic dentist, she brings a wealth of expertise to enhance your oral health and smile. Whether it's restoring your dental well-being or crafting your dream smile, trust in her years of experience and commitment to excellence.
